    Introducing Freelancer

    Users input specific measurements into the AI system, ensuring precision for a custom-fit garment.

    The AI transforms the provided measurements into a detailed 2D pattern, outlining the initial design on a flat surface.

    Using NLP-processed, eco-friendly measurements, the 2D pattern is elevated into a 3D model, showcasing the cloth design from all angles.

    The 3D garment can be rotated and viewed in a 360-degree virtual space, allowing for a comprehensive examination of its fit and style.

    Detailed explanations of fabric type, texture, and other minute details are displayed adjacent to the 3D model for clarity and better understanding.
